BG: Siege of Dragonspear -> BG2 Skie Silvershield Resolution / Closure.
Mod for Baldur's Gate 2: Enhanced Edition

version: 0.9 beta

This mod is released as a functional beta.


Scope:
* Provides a clean closure to Skie Silvershield’s murder from BG1EE: Siege of Dragonspear.
* Designed to integrate into a standard BG2EE playthrough.

Compatibility:
* Incompatible with any mod that provides a resolution to Skie Silvershield’s fate in BG2.
* The Price of One Girl’s Soul is explicitly detected and will block installation.
* Other Skie-related mods are not checked and may result in conflicting behavior if installed.

Out of scope:
* Full coverage of all edge cases.
* Compatibility with every mod combination.
* Handling of deliberately broken or unusual player states.

Bug reports:
* Please report only issues that block progression or clearly break the quest.
* Minor inconsistencies or edge cases may not be addressed.

The goal is a stable, minimal, lore-consistent closure - not exhaustive system coverage.


If you import a character from a Siege of Dragonspear final save, you will find an item
in Irenicus’ Dungeon that allows you to resolve the fate of Skie Silvershield’s soul.

Once acquired, follow the journal.
The quest is intentionally short, and the required NPC should be easy to identify.

Note: the quest must be initiated before siding with Bodhi.
If already in progress, it can still be completed after switching sides.
